FAQs

How do I search for exams?

You can search for exams using the search bar at the top of the page. Enter keywords related to the exam title, subject, or creator to find relevant exams.

Can I create my own exams?

Yes! After signing up, you can create your own exams by clicking on the "Create Exam" button in the navigation menu.

Is there a way to track my progress?

Yes! Once you're logged in, you can access your dashboard to view your exam history, scores, and track your progress over time.
